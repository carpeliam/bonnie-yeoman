measure period: 2012
CMS104v2:
Discharged on Antithrombotic Therapy
Measure Settings
Description:

Ischemic stroke patients prescribed antithrombotic therapy at hospital discharge

Initial Patient Population:
toggle Initial Patient Population

  • AND : Patient Characteristic Birthdate: birth date (status: covered) >= 18 years starts before start of Occurrence A: Encounter, Performed: Inpatient Encounter (status: covered)
  • AND : Occurrence A: Encounter, Performed: Inpatient Encounter ( Length of Stay <= 120 days ) (status: covered)
  • AND : Occurrence A: Encounter, Performed: Inpatient Encounter ( Discharge Date/Time ) (status: covered) during "Measurement Period"
  • AND :
    • OR : Diagnosis, Active: Hemorrhagic Stroke ( Ordinal : Principal Diagnosis ) (status: covered) starts during Occurrence A: Encounter, Performed: Inpatient Encounter (status: covered)
    • OR : Diagnosis, Active: Ischemic Stroke ( Ordinal : Principal Diagnosis ) (status: not covered) starts during Occurrence A: Encounter, Performed: Inpatient Encounter (status: covered)

Denominator:
toggle Denominator

None

    • OR : Occurrence A: Encounter, Performed: Inpatient Encounter ( Discharge Status : Discharge To Another Hospital ) (status: not covered)
    • OR : Occurrence A: Encounter, Performed: Inpatient Encounter ( Discharge Status : Left Against Medical Advice ) (status: not covered)
    • OR : Occurrence A: Encounter, Performed: Inpatient Encounter ( Discharge Status : Discharged To Home For Hospice Care ) (status: not covered)
    • OR : Occurrence A: Encounter, Performed: Inpatient Encounter ( Discharge Status : Patient Expired ) (status: not covered)

      • AND :
        • OR :

        • OR :

      • OR : Intervention, Performed: Palliative Care (status: not covered) starts during Occurrence A: Encounter, Performed: Inpatient Encounter (status: not covered)
